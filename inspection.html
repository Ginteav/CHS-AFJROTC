Inspection


What is inspection?

Inspection is a formal evaluation each AFJROTC unit undergoes. The purpose is to assess whether each unit is meeting standards.

What can you expect?

Expect to do a larger scale inspection, similar to the ones done on uniform days, but rather than being a class inspection done by your flight commander, it is the entire corps being evaluated by the inspector. Ensure you know the Air Force core values, and general AFJROTC standards. Take your uniform to the dry cleaners if you can, and ensure your haircut is within regulations. Females should have their hair pulled back, preferably into a neat bun, and jewellry should stay within regulations. Please read through The Cadet Handbook, specifically Chapter 8 and uniform relevant attatchments. Inspection is mandatory for cadets, and will be done during the school day at some point, with school leave given.

When is Inspection?

January 28th.
