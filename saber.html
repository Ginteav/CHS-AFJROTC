Saber Team

What Is a Saber Team?

A saber team is a group of cadets who are capable of performing intricate drill routines using ceremonial sabers for various different military events and ceremonies. Our saber team upholds and showcases discipline and teamwork in order to go above, and beyond, the standards.

What Events Does Our Team Participate in?

Our saber team consistently is involved in assisting Choctawhatchee High Schoool's Homecoming court, the Christmas Parade, and our JROTC unit's Military Ball. We have a variety of other events throughout the year, and we frequently collaborate with other special teams to add some extra flare.
